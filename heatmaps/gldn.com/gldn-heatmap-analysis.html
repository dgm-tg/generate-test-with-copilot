User Attention Flow

The typical user attention flow on GLDN.com follows this pattern:

  1. Logo/branding recognition
  2. Hero headline and main CTA
  3. First product category or featured product
  4. Horizontal scanning of top categories
  5. Secondary banner content
  6. Best-selling products (left to right, decreasing attention)
  7. Brand story heading (but less attention on the content)
  8. Social proof/Instagram section
  9. Brief footer scan (primarily newsletter signup)

Optimization Recommendations

  1. Prioritize hero CTA placement - Ensure it aligns with the highest attention area
  2. Position key products on the left - Take advantage of the natural F-pattern
  3. Enhance visual hierarchy - Use size, color and contrast to guide attention flow
  4. Simplify lower-attention areas - Reduce clutter in footer and secondary sections
  5. Test alternative placements - Particularly for secondary CTAs and promotions
